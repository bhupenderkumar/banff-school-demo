Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is designed for children aged 3-5, and provides a nurturing environment that fosters social, emotional, and cognitive development. Our experienced teachers use play-based learning strategies to engage young minds and prepare them for success in kindergarten.

📚

Elementary

In our elementary program, students in grades K-4 engage in a rigorous and engaging curriculum that includes language arts, mathematics, science, and social studies. Our small class sizes and experienced teachers ensure that every child receives the individual attention they need to thrive.

📚

Middle/Upper

Our middle and upper school programs serve students in grades 5-8, and provide a challenging and supportive learning environment that prepares students for high school and beyond. Our faculty uses a range of innovative strategies and technologies to engage students and help them achieve their full potential.

🔬

STEM

At Banff School, we believe that science, technology, engineering, and math are essential skills for success in the 21st century. Our STEM program provides students with hands-on experiences and real-world applications that foster curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking.

🎨

Arts

Our arts program offers students the opportunity to explore their creativity and express themselves through music, visual arts, drama, and dance. Our experienced teachers provide guidance and support as students develop their skills and talents.

⚽️

Athletics

At Banff School, we believe that physical activity and sports are essential for a healthy and balanced lifestyle. Our athletic program offers students the opportunity to engage in team sports, individual sports, and fitness activities that promote teamwork, discipline, and perseverance.
